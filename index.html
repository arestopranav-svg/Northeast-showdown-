Tournament Rules

Read carefully before registering

  • 01

    Team Composition

    Each team must consist of exactly 4 players. Substitutes are not allowed mid-tournament unless authorized by admins.

  • 02

    Fair Play Policy

    Any use of hacks, mods, or third-party software will result in immediate disqualification and permanent ban.

  • 03

    Registration Fee

    Payment must be completed before submission. Upload clear screenshot of payment for verification.

  • 04

    Check-in Requirement

    Teams must check-in 30 minutes before match start time. Failure to check-in results in forfeit.

  • 05

    Device & Network

    Players are responsible for their own device and internet connection. No rematches for technical issues.
